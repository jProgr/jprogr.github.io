# Days of the week in Elefen

- Lundi: Monday.
- Martedi: Tuesday.
- Mercurdi: Wednesday.
- Jovedi: Thursday.
- Venerdi: Friday.
- Saturdi: Saturday.
- Soldi: Sunday.

Remember that the days of the week are written with the lower case when inside of a sentence according to the Elefen grammar.
