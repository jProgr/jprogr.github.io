# Landforms in Mini

![](/assets/images/mini_lan_figura.png)

Landforms and land features:

- bai - bay, gulf, cove.
- delta - delta.
- farina-monte - dune.
- fen - fen, swamp, bog, marsh.
- foreta - forest.
- ila - island.
- ila-junta - archipelago.
- ise-lan - iceberg.
- kalori-fonte - geyser.
- kanali - canal.
- kave - cave.
- kolina - hill.
- kula-foreta - tundra.
- kula-vali - fjord, fiord.
- lago - lake.
- lan-figura - landform.
- lan-tumo - cape.
- litora - shore, beach, coast.
- mare - ocean, sea.
- mare-lago - lagoon.
- mega-ise-lan - glacier.
- mega-tingi-foli-lan - plateau.
- monte - mountain.
- naro-lan - isthmus.
- naro-vasa - strait.
- riva - river, stream.
- saara - desert.
- semi-ila - peninsula.
- subita-lan - cliff, precipice, barranca, barranco.
- tingi-foli-lan - mesa.
- unda-go-vasa - fall (of liquid), waterfall, cascade, cataract (of water).
- vali - valley.
