# The human body in Mini

![](/assets/images/mini_bodi.png)

Body:

- man bodi - human body.
- kapo - head.
- fore - forehead.
- oko - eye.
- mimi - ear.
- nase - nose.
- boka - mouth.
- neka - neck.
- kanda - shoulder.
- sina - chest.
- beli - stomach.
- rama - arm.
- mano - hand.
- diji - finger.
- noga - leg.
- kolenka - knee.
- talon - heel.
- pedi - foot.
