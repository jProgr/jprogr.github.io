# The months in Elefen

- Janero: January.
- Febrero: February.
- Marto: March.
- April: April.
- Maio: May.
- Junio: June.
- Julio: July.
- Agosto: August.
- Setembre: September.
- Otobre: October.
- Novembre: November.
- Desembre: December.

Remember that the months are written with the lower case when inside of a sentence according to the Elefen grammar.
