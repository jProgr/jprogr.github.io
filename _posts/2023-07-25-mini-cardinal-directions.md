# Cardinal directions in Mini

![](/assets/images/mini_kapo_direto.png)

Cardinal directions:

- nore - north.
- sude - south.
- ese - east.
- vese - west.
- nore-ese - northeast.
- nore-vese - northwest.
- sude-ese - southeast.
- sude-vese - southwest.
- kapo direto - cardinal directions.
