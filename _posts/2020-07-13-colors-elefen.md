# The colors in Elefen

There are many colors but here are some of the basic ones in Elefen:

- Negra: black.
- Brun: brown.
- Gris: grey.
- Blanca: white.
- Jala: yellow.
- Orania: orange.
- Roja: red.
- Ros: pink.
- Purpur: purple.
- Blu: blue.
- Verde: green.
